Prototype Preview

To-GoRing NFC Keyring

A small, always-with-you keyring that becomes your “record” button. Tap on your phone to start a mini diary in seconds.

From friction to flow. Digital diaries are often opened too late – when the feelings have already faded. We remove the unlock–open–type friction and keep the emotion of “right now”.
Problem

Why we built To-GoLing

Modern life moves fast. By the time you open your notes app at home, the vivid feeling from that café, river, or bus ride is gone.

Friction

Too many steps to record

Unlock phone → find app → open → type. That tiny delay is enough for people to give up recording their day.

Emotion

Feelings fade quickly

“I’ll write about it later” turns into “I don’t remember exactly”. Precious moments are lost or flattened into generic text.

Context

Location is missing

Traditional diaries rarely connect feelings with concrete places, routes, and real-world context – even though those matter.

Solution

A diary that starts with a tap

To-GoLing combines a physical NFC keyring (“To-GoRing”) with an intelligent app (“To-Go”) to turn your everyday movement into an effortless life log.

Hardware

To-GoRing: emotional NFC keyring

Attach it to your bag, keys, or AirPods. The keyring acts as your record trigger – a tiny ritual that feels analog and warm.

  • NFC-based trigger to open the app
  • Always on you, zero extra steps
  • Designed as a fashion item, not a gadget
Software

To-Go: context-aware diary app

The app turns your tap into a structured micro-entry with location, time, and guiding questions – no blank page anxiety.

  • Auto-captures place & weather
  • AI prompts tailored to your location
  • Map & feed view of your day
Flow

How it works in 3 steps

We keep your life’s flow intact. Recording should feel like a heartbeat, not homework.

Step 1

Tap the keyring

When you feel something worth remembering, simply tap the To-GoRing on your phone – wherever you are.

Step 2

Answer a tiny question

The app opens with a short, location-based question like “You’re at the river. What song are you listening to?”

Step 3

See your day as a map

Entries appear on both a feed and a map. Later, you can revisit not just what you felt, but where you felt it.

Extra

To-Go List: location-based to-do

You can set “to-go” tasks that only complete when you actually visit the place and tap your keyring.
Example: “Arrive at the library by 10:00.” When you get there and tag, you get a small celebration and character XP.

Team

About Team 12V

To-GoLing is a one-time university project exploring how hardware and software can blend into a more human way of recording life.

Project Scope

Prototype, not a startup (yet)

This version of To-GoLing is a proof-of-concept focused on:

  • NFC keyring prototype design
  • Core app UX & interaction flow
  • Early user feedback & demo
Vision

From diary to life log

We see a world where diaries are alive with places, routes, and honest moments – not just text written before bed.

Project timeline
Phase 1 – Research & Ideation: interviews with digital diary users and analysis of “friction” in recording.
Phase 2 – Prototype: NFC keyring mock-up, app flow, and early tests of “tap to log”.
Phase 3 – Demo: in-class presentation, user feedback, and reflection on what worked – and what to try next.
